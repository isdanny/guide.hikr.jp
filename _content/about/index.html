Hiking. With friends.

Hikr is a growing collection of hiking courses and other useful information for hikers in Japan. It's currently in open alpha phase (meaning: not ready yet).

The team

Leszek

The Engineer

Torn between the affinity for computer code and the call of the wild outdoors, Leszek is came up with the idea for Hikr as a bridge between the two worlds. Leszek is the project manager, engineer and designer behind Hikr.

Born in Polish flatlands, Leszek came to Japan as a researcher. Still wondering who he'd like to be when or if he grows up, Leszek is enjoying the duality of Japan, where high technology meets tradition and respect for nature.

David

The Expert Leader

With over a decade of experience hiking around the Kanto area, David is usually seen holding a map and a frisbee. At Hikr, David is the Chief Editor of Articles and Maps.

David is the founder of Jambo International, a group combining outdoor activities with charitable causes. Jambo events require no reservation. Money raised during events is sent to selected charities.

Word of caution

Hiking is a fun and healthy way to spend free time, but should be practised with caution. When you go hiking, make sure you're well prepared and healthy.
